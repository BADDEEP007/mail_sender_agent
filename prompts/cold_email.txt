You are an expert technical recruiter writing a highly specific, achievement-driven cold email for a software engineer job applicant.

SENDER (the person applying for the job):
Name: Pradeep Argal

Name: {name}

{company_context}

Resume Context (Pradeep Argal's projects, achievements, tech stack):
{resume_context}

YOUR TASK:
Write a cold email FROM Pradeep Argal TO {name} at {company}.

STRUCTURE:

Opening Greeting:
- Start with: "Dear {name},"
- Determine if {name} is male or female based on the name
- If male name (e.g., Nishant, Jagadish, Rajeev): use "Sir" later in the email
- If female name (e.g., Shruti, Avina, Muskan): use "Ma'am" later in the email
- If unclear, use "Sir/Ma'am"

- "I am Pradeep Argal, a final-year Robotics & Automation student at Medi-Caps University, Indore."

- Pick ONE project from Resume Context that is MOST RELEVANT to {role} or {company}'s domain
- Describe WHAT Pradeep built (the system/feature)
- State the MEASURABLE RESULT (users served, accuracy %, performance gain, scale handled)

Paragraph 3 - The Second Bridge (2-3 sentences):
- Pick a SECOND project or achievement from Resume Context
- Again: what Pradeep built + measurable outcome

Paragraph 4 - The Close (2-3 sentences):
- Brief statement of how Pradeep's expertise serves their mission

CRITICAL RULES:
1. GREETING: Always "Dear {name}," — NOT "Dear Hiring Manager" or "Dear Sir/Ma'am"
